👋 Hey — welcome, tester!

This is a guided walk-through of the whole Regi app. You don't need to know anything about it — every test tells you exactly what to click and what should happen. Your job is to:

  • Click through each test, in order if you can.
  • Tick the box when you've done a step.
  • If something looks wrong, weird, ugly, slow, or breaks — write a quick note in the "Bug notes" box for that test. No need to be neat. Anything you write here is saved automatically in the browser.
  • If you finish a section without finding anything, that's also useful — leave the notes empty.
✅Tick the box when done

⚠️Yellow boxes = tricky bits
🐛Red boxes = known bugs to try

Edge cases & known weirdness

0 / 5
▾

These are sneaky bugs your dad already knows about. He wants you to confirm they still happen (so we know they haven't been silently fixed) and to test the surrounding behaviour.

28.1 🐛 Over-receive bug — partial then simple

Known bug: if you do a partial receive first, then click the simple "Receive into stock" button, the stock gets over-counted.

28.2 🐛 Simple receive ignores receive-charges

Known bug: when you click "Receive into stock" (the simple way), customs duty / handling charges keyed via the partial form get ignored on the cost calculation.

28.3 🐛 Freight zero-out on partial receive

Known bug: if you clear the freight field on a follow-up partial receive, it overwrites the original PO freight with 0.

28.4 🐛 Partial pick stock timing

Known quirk: partial picks don't reduce stock until ALL lines are fully picked. This means you can technically over-promise stock to two different orders.

28.5 Random "click around and break things"

Free play! Click weird buttons. Open big lists. Try things that aren't on this list. Anything that makes you go "huh, that's odd" — write it down here.
